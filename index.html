FAQs

What is Elympaid?
Elympiad is application developed by Indic Arena where you can earn Real Money by using your knowledge. Everyday trivia get published and on that questions get asked in the Quiz. It is totally mcq base quiz.

It is not only money earning app but also learning app. You can learn more about different topics daily and expand your knowledge. If you are preparing for the competitive exams this is for you.
Can I withdraw the bonus cash/Indic Coins I get after signing up?
No, the Indic Coins can be used as entry fees for entering quizes.
What will my friend/contact have to do for me to get the bonus cash from referrals?
Your friend will have to sign up on Elympiad either with your referral link or using your referral code during login.
Will I be able to withdraw the bonus cash I make from referrals?
No, the Indic Coins can be used as entry fees for entering quizes.
Is there any tax on my winnings?
If winning amount is greater than 10,000 Rs then we will deduct TDS ( tax deducted at source) as per the government regulation.

As per current regulations, Tax will be calculated as follows i.e 30% tax + 4% cess which equals down to 31.2% on the won amount for any amount above 10,000 Rs won in a Quiz.
When do I get my winnings?
Whenever you withdraw winnings it may take upto 2-3 business days to process the transaction.
What are Deposit money, Winnings and Indic coins?
Deposit money : Money which you deposit in the wallet. You can use this to participate in the quiz. You can't withdraw this Amount from wallet.

Winnings : Money which you win by participating in the quizes. You can withdraw this money whenever you want.

Indic Coins : Sign-up and refferal bonus are called as Indic Coins. You can use this Coins to participate in the quizes.

Can I have multiple accounts?
No, you cannot have multiple profiles. 1 person - 1 profile. At any point in time, if we detect that you have multiple ids or are misusing the system in anyway, your account may get terminated, and all money that you may have earned will be forfeited. You may also be banned from using the application.
What will happen if I am not able to join the quiz in given time?
You will not get any refund if you are not able to join quiz in given time.
I want to delete my account, how to do it?
You can Contact us via Discord. Create ticket and our staff will look into this.
